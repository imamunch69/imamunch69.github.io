The black ops 2 campaign takes place in the year 2025. It starts off
with David Mason looking for a veteran named Frank Woods. Woods tells
Mason about his military career alongside David's father, Alex Mason.
For this reason, the game switches between two time periods, the past
and the future. Both periods, however, feature the antagonist Raul
Menendez, which connects it together. The game continues the captivating
story started in Black Ops 1, and innovates by adding a new mechanic,
being able to make choices that actually impact the story, and giving
different endings. For this reason, the campaign in Black Ops 2 is
extremely captivating, and lives up to the standard set by it's predecessor.
